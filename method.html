The BentiBuild Method

Learn how we transform plastic bottles into sturdy homes using simple, community-based techniques.

Construction Steps

1. Bottle Collection

Community members collect and clean plastic bottles from local sources.

2. Bottle Preparation

Bottles are filled with sand or soil and sealed to create building blocks.

3. Wall Construction

Bottles are arranged in rows and secured with wire or mud to form walls.

4. Roofing and Finishing

Add roofing materials and plaster the walls for a finished home.
